Our Mission

SimpleSource's mission is to empower seniors by providing a user-friendly online library of technology guides and resources. We aim to enhance digital literacy among older adults while enabling student volunteers to contribute educational content. By bridging the digital divide, SimpleSource helps seniors confidently navigate technology and stay connected in today's digital world.
